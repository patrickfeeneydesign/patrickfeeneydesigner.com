PORTFOLIO PROJECT

Creating an Anologue Footprint

As a part of my plans for summer 2022, I set out to create a brand new striking portfolio. The concept behind the publication is a 'Love Letter to Design,' placing the viewer in the position of an archivist discovering old love letters from a distant past. I designed and printed the work at home, using materials from my bedroom and utlising a cut-and-paste aesthetic.

Often times, designers can get stuck in digital formats and forget how powerful our home printers really are! Working on this project has reminded me to cherish my home printer and welcome lo-fi techniques in the future.
